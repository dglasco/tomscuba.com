2 The Tom's journey

One pool. A lifetime of water.

Most shops sell you a class. Tom's grows with you. Here's what the next ten years can look like, if you let them.

Year 1, 6 mo+

Parent & child class

Your first time in our 91° pool. Floating, splashing, building trust. The earliest step on the longest journey.

Ages 4–12

Stroke development & summer camps

Group classes, SSI curriculum, small groups. Confidence becomes a stroke becomes a swimmer.

Ages 10+

SSI Open Water certification

The moment you exhale and watch the bubbles rise. Pool sessions in Austin, open water at Lake Travis or Cozumel.

Year 2+

Specialties: Nitrox, photography, wrecks

Perfect Buoyancy. Stress & Rescue. Underwater Photography. The shape of the diver you're becoming.

Year 3+

Austin Aquanauts & global travel

Monthly socials, statewide dives, and group trips to Bonaire, Roatán, Galápagos. The community you didn't know you were looking for.

Year 5+

Divemaster & beyond

For some, the journey loops back. They start teaching. Tom's has put more dive professionals in the water than anyone in Central Texas.
