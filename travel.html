3 What's included

What you're actually paying for.

Every trip is different but the philosophy is the same: we book the things you'd book anyway, get them for less than you would, and bundle them so you don't think about logistics for a week. Specific inclusions are detailed in each trip package.

Always included

  • Lodging (typically dive resort or equivalent)
  • All boat-based diving (typically 2–3 tanks/day)
  • Daily breakfast at the resort
  • Airport pickup and dropoff
  • Tom's trip leader on the ground
  • Group welcome dinner and farewell dinner
  • Dive marine park fees where applicable

Not included (typically)

  • International airfare
  • Travel insurance (strongly recommended)
  • Most lunches and dinners
  • Gear rental (we can arrange in advance)
  • Nitrox fills (typically + $100–200/week)
  • Crew gratuities
  • Optional excursions (sharks, special dives)
