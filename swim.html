5 Common questions

Questions parents always ask.

Do parents get in the water with the kids?
Only for our Parent & Child class (6 months to 2 years). Starting with Preschool 1, kids are in the water directly with the instructor while parents watch from our dedicated viewing area.
How warm is the pool, really?
91 to 92 degrees, year-round. It's small (10 yards) and indoors, maintained by our on-staff Certified Pool Operator. Cold pools teach kids to avoid water. Ours doesn't.
What if my child misses a class?
Group class students can schedule a make-up lesson through our mobile app. We try to honor every legitimate miss — life happens.
Do you teach adults?
Yes — through private appointment only. Many adults come to us before a beach vacation, a triathlon, or just to finally learn. No judgment, just water and patience.
When do summer camps open for registration?
Spring Break and Summer Camp registration typically opens in late January and February. Sign up for our newsletter or follow us on Facebook to be notified — they fill quickly.
Is there a mobile app?
Yes. Our swim school app lets you enroll, check attendance, book privates, and get push notifications for announcements. Available on iOS and Android.

Ready to get them swimming?

Walk in any weekday between 10am and 6:30pm. Or get in touch to enroll. The hardest part is the first lesson — after that, they'll be asking when they get to come back.
