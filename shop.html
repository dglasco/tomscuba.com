Service & repair

Equipment repair

Factory-trained technicians for all major manufacturers — Aqua Lung, Apeks, Atomic, Sherwood, Spare Air, Suunto, OTS, AGA, and Whites. Flat-rate servicing with no time limits: every piece gets the time it needs, rebuilt to manufacturer spec with approved annual service kits.

  • Full regulator rebuilds: ultrasonic cleaning, new manufacturer parts, 3000 psi leak test
  • BCD inspection and bladder repair
  • Mail-in service via UPS/FedEx — we service customers across the region
  • Trusted by Navy, Coast Guard, fire, and police dive teams
