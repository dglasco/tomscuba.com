1 The diver's path

Five fathoms of training.
One depth at a time.

Diving is sequential. You can't lead a wreck dive before you can hover. Tom's takes you through each level deliberately — most divers progress one cert per year, exactly when they're ready.

Level 1 · The start

Open Water Diver

Your first certification. Pool sessions in Austin, open water dives at our home lakes or in the Caribbean. The cert most divers ever need.

Level 2 · Confidence

Advanced & Specialties

Perfect Buoyancy, Nitrox, Deep, Wreck, Photography. Stack the skills that turn certified divers into competent ones.

Level 3 · Mastery

Stress & Rescue

The course every serious diver should take. Manage your own stress, recognize it in others, respond when it matters.

Level 4 · Leadership

Divemaster

The first professional rating. Assist on courses, lead certified divers, work the boat. Where the journey gets serious.

Level 5 · Teach

Instructor & beyond

Become the person who puts new divers in the water. Tom's has produced more dive professionals than anyone in Central Texas.

2 Start here

Begin your diving life.

Curious but not committed? Start with Try Scuba. Ready? Open Water runs year-round in weeknight and weekend formats — pool sessions in our heated indoor pool, checkout dives at Lake Travis or Spring Lake in San Marcos. One heads-up: every student passes a watermanship check (200m swim + 10-minute tread) on day one. Need help with that? We know a good swim school.
