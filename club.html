1 The story

Three Tom's regulars,
twelve years ago.

In 2013, a few of Tom's most loyal divers got tired of trying to coordinate local dives by group text. They started a club. Twelve years later, the Austin Aquanauts is 200+ members strong — a real community with monthly socials, exclusive trips, and the kind of people who'll meet you at 5am to drive to Lake Travis.

2 Member benefits

What you get
when you join.

Membership pays for itself within a year, usually faster if you take a course or go on a trip. But the discounts aren't the point — the community is. That part doesn't have a price tag.

i

Member pricing on courses and gear

10% off all SSI courses and specialty classes. 10% off most retail gear in the shop (excludes already-discounted items and a few brand exclusions).

ii

Free air fills

Members get free standard air fills on the day of each club dive. Nitrox fills at a discounted member rate.

iii

Presale on international trips

Aquanauts get access to trip registration two weeks before the public. Our most popular trips (Cozumel, Galápagos, Fiji) often sell out during presale.

iv

Monthly socials

Last Friday of every month, at Tom's or a rotating Austin venue. Photo nights, trip recaps, gear demos, guest presentations, and the world's best dive stories.

v

Local dive days

Quarterly group dives at Lake Travis, Lake Buchanan, Blue Lagoon, and Athens Scuba Park. Air fills included. Bring your own gear or rent at member rates.

vi

The WhatsApp group

The single most useful benefit, hands down. Trip pickup buddies, gear recommendations, dive-buddy matching, and a steady stream of underwater photos that will make you book your next trip.
