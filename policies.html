Privacy Policy

Sample · last updated June 2026

Tom's Dive & Swim ("we," "us") operates tomsscuba.com, shop.tomsscuba.com, and austinswim.com. This policy describes what we collect and how we use it.

What we collect. When you contact us, enroll in a class, join the club, or make a purchase, we collect the information you provide: name, email, phone, and — for purchases — payment and shipping details processed by our point-of-sale provider. For scuba students, we also collect required training records and medical statements per SSI standards.

Analytics & cookies. We use Google Tag Manager and Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site (pages visited, approximate location, device type). These tools set cookies. We do not sell personal information to anyone.

How we use it. To deliver services you've requested, process orders, maintain training records, send class and trip communications, and — only if you opt in — send our newsletter. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email.

Children. Our swim school serves children, but enrollment, accounts, and payment are always handled by a parent or guardian. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 online.

Your choices. To access, correct, or delete your information, email sales@tomsscuba.com or call (512) 451-3425.

Safety & Medical Requirements

Based on SSI standards & published course requirements
  • All scuba students complete the SSI medical statement before in-water training; some answers require a physician's sign-off before you can begin.
  • Open Water students must pass a watermanship check on day one: a 200-meter swim and a 10-minute tread.
  • Minimum age for Open Water certification is 10. Swimmers under 2 participate only in Parent & Child classes with a parent in the water.
  • Certification cards (any agency) are required for tank fills, rentals of life-support equipment, and participation in dives and trips.
  • All participants in classes, dives, and trips sign liability releases before in-water activities.
  • We reserve the right to remove any participant from in-water activities for safety reasons, with fees handled per the relevant cancellation policy.
